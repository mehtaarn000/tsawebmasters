What's the deal?

Green, renewable energy is the cheapest power option due to the consistently dropping prices. Between 2010 and 2020 the cost of implementing solar power dropped around 80%.

 

Humans should switch to green energy as it is imperative to mitigate the impacts of climate change, as it significantly effects the health of humans. The use of traditional fossil fuels is a major contributing factor towards climate change. Additionally, green energy sources are renewable and sustainable, ensuring a long-term energy solution for future generations. Making use of green energy also facilitates technology innovation and economic growth.

 

Any goals?

The IRENA(The International Renewable Energy Agency) suggests that 90% of the world's energy should come from green energy by the year 2050.
